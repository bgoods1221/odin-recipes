Sous Vide New York Strip

Description

The real advantage to this technique is the fact that you're guaranteeing a perfect medium-rare. If you've never had any luck getting large, expensive hunks of steak cooked to your idea of perfection, then this is the way to go.

Ingredients

  • 2 new york strip steaks
  • salt and pepper
  • vegetables oil
  • butter

Steps

  1. Heat water to 134 degrees
  2. Vac seal steaks and season
  3. Add sealed steaks into water bath when temp reaches 134
  4. Cook for 1.5 hours and take out of sealed bag when done
  5. Heat cast iron pan rippin' hot
  6. Sear steaks. 15-30 seconds per side. A minute and a half total
